Forecasted Yield by Batch

Forecast Methodology

Formula:

Forecasted Yield = (Boards Transplanted - Major Damage Boards) × Expected lbs/board × (1 - % Stunted/Deformed)

This calculation accounts for complete board losses from major damage and reduced productivity from stunted/deformed plants.
